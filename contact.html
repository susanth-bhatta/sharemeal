Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a food pickup?

You can schedule a food pickup through our mobile app or website. Simply go to the "Schedule Pickup" section, select your location, choose a date and time, and provide details about the food you're donating. Our team will confirm the pickup within 24 hours.

What types of food can I donate?

We accept non-perishable food items as well as fresh produce that is still safe for consumption. This includes canned goods, dry pasta, rice, beans, unopened packaged foods, fresh fruits and vegetables, and baked goods. We cannot accept expired items, open packages, or homemade meals without proper labeling.

How can I become a volunteer?

We'd love to have you as a volunteer! Please visit the "Volunteer" section in your profile and complete the application form. After a brief background check and orientation session, you'll be ready to help with food collection, sorting, or distribution in your local community.

How is my donation distributed?

All donations are sorted and distributed to local food banks, shelters, and community organizations within 24-48 hours of collection. We prioritize organizations serving vulnerable populations including homeless shelters, women's shelters, and children's programs.

Can I get a tax receipt for my donation?

Yes, we provide tax receipts for all food donations. After your donation is received and processed, you'll receive an email with a digital receipt that includes the estimated value of your donation. For large donations, we can provide a detailed valuation report upon request.
